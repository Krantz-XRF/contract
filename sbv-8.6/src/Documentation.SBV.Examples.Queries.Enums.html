-- | Days of the week. We make it symbolic using the 'mkSymbolicEnumeration' splice.
data Day = Monday | Tuesday | Wednesday | Thursday | Friday | Saturday | Sunday

-- | Make 'Day' a symbolic value.

-- and others.)
